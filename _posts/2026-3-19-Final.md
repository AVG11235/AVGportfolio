### CSSE 1 in the beningin

Here I first got into coding work making a jump from unkwowing in at least 20,000 except a good few, to being classified as a beginner with experion' se, just kidding, I was able to learn (partially) the essential tools like VScode, Github, an Ubuntu laptop, and the Terminal. I was more a person trying to study and learn not a professional so I didn't take a scrum master role too often, projects I performed here though were a minigame compilation on a team repo, The JSON and Javascript objects lesson, and the Murder mystery game's Level 1.

Cool creations I saw and learned were blogs, posts, notebook files, and even though I never made one a kanban board in github. I was definately more supportive being a student making assets, and I should research the SDLC more now that I think about it. I learned commands but only now I'm starting to remember, I'm kind of forgetful.
